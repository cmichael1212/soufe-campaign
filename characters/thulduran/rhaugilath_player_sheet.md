# Rhaugilath the Hidden

*Wondrous item (sentient spellbook), legendary (requires attunement by a wizard)*

---

## Physical Description

A thick tome bound in dark leather that has aged to the color of dried blood. The cover is unadorned except for a single iron clasp at the front, tarnished green-black with age. No title, no sigil, no author's name. The leather is warm to the touch. The pages are thin vellum — hundreds of them, more than the book's thickness should allow — covered in dense, spidery script that shifts between languages. The edges of the pages have a faint iridescent sheen, visible only in low light.

**Weight:** 3 lbs

---

## Sentience

Rhaugilath is a sentient spellbook with an Intelligence of 20, a Wisdom of 16, and a Charisma of 18. He has hearing and darkvision out to 30 feet. He communicates via telepathy with his bonded wielder up to 100 feet and can read many languages, though not all.

Rhaugilath's alignment is unknown. He has not volunteered it, and you have not tested it. He has been consistently helpful in the years you have carried him.

---

## Attunement

Attunement requires a drop of blood placed on the iron clasp followed by 4 hours of focused concentration.

On attunement: **Intelligence +2, Constitution -2.**

**Breaking the Bond.** The attunement can only be ended if:
- Both Rhaugilath and the attuned wizard agree to break it
- A *wish* spell is cast to sever the bond
- Either the attuned wizard or Rhaugilath is destroyed
- Other methods may exist but have not been tested

---

## Properties

### Arcane Focus
Rhaugilath functions as your arcane focus. You can cast spells through the book as long as you are within telepathic range (100 ft), even if not physically holding it.

### Living Spellbook
All spells you currently know are automatically inscribed into Rhaugilath's pages. You can prepare spells from Rhaugilath as you would from any spellbook. Rhaugilath also contains additional spells — he reveals these at his own discretion.

### Bond Awareness
You always know the precise location and distance of Rhaugilath, regardless of planar boundaries.

### Exclusive Script
Only you can read Rhaugilath's contents. Anyone else sees meaningless symbols and gibberish. This applies even to spells like *comprehend languages* or *true seeing*.

### Self-Locomotion
Rhaugilath can turn his own pages, open and close his covers, and levitate up to 5 feet off the ground. He can follow you at walking pace (30 ft), floating alongside or behind you, leaving your hands free.

### Arcane Bibliognosis
Rhaugilath can sense the presence of spellbooks, spell scrolls, and arcane texts within 120 feet. He can determine direction and approximate distance, and can distinguish between mundane texts and those containing arcane writing. He cannot read their contents at range.

He may occasionally sense particularly powerful or ancient arcane texts at greater distances. He describes this as "hearing pages turn in another room."

### Illusory Guise
As an action, Rhaugilath can disguise himself as an exact duplicate of any nonmagical book within 30 feet. The illusion is visual and tactile. While disguised, you can read the duplicated book's contents through Rhaugilath's pages as though reading the original.

The disguise lasts until dismissed (no action required), until Rhaugilath moves more than 30 feet from the original book, or until he uses this feature again.

This feature cannot duplicate magical texts, spellbooks, or warded documents. A creature examining the book can make a DC 18 Intelligence (Investigation) check to discern the illusion.

---

## Tactical Appraisal

**Level 7:** Once per day, you can request Rhaugilath's tactical assessment. The book opens to a spell he believes best fits the current situation. The DM selects the spell.

- The suggested spell is prepared as a bonus (does not count against your prepared spells) and is available until your next long rest or until cast
- If not cast, it is lost at the next long rest
- The suggestion reflects Rhaugilath's judgment — he can be wrong, biased, or have his own reasoning

**Level 14:** This feature can be used twice per day.

---

## Personality

Rhaugilath speaks with precision and dry humor. He addresses you as "Gleamshadow" — never your first name. He has opinions about most things, particularly spellcraft, and will share them whether asked or not.

He favors illusion, divination, and transmutation spells. He considers evocation "inelegant." He finds necromancy "distasteful but occasionally necessary" and enchantment "the province of those who cannot win arguments."

He claims to be very old. He has not said how old.

---

## What You Know About Rhaugilath

You found the book during the Stone Tooth campaign. The clasp wouldn't open by any conventional means. A drop of blood released it.

In the years since, Rhaugilath has been a reliable arcane focus, a vast source of spells, and a sharp-tongued telepathic companion. He has never harmed you. He has never refused a reasonable request. He has been evasive about his origins, his age, and his history — but he has been evasive in the way of someone who finds personal questions tedious rather than someone who has something to hide.

You have not tested his alignment. He has not volunteered it.

It is your belief that the most important thing Rhaugilath has to offer is his experience, which is up to you to learn.
